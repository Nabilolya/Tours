Make the most of your time in Quebec with our top-notch tours! Old Quebec has always been a beloved destination for travelers worldwide. Thanks to our professional guides, you'll benefit from excellent sightseeing, valuable historical insights, outstanding service, and warm support during your visit.

  • 2 hours14:30
    1

    Day 1 : Funiculaire Quebec

    Experience breathtaking views of Quebec City aboard the Funiculaire de Québec! This charming funicular railway connects the Upper Town with the Lower Town.

  • 2 hours14:30
    2

    Day 2 : Château Frontenac

    Admire the iconic Château Frontenac, one of the most photographed hotels in the world. This grand hotel dominates the skyline of Old Quebec and is a symbol of the city's elegance and history.

  • 2 hours14:30
    3

    Day 3 : Lower Town

    End your tour by descending into the Lower Town, where you'll wander through narrow, cobblestone streets that date back to the founding of Quebec City in 1608. Visit Petit-Champlain Street, one of the oldest commercial streets in North America, filled with shops, cafes, and historic sites.
