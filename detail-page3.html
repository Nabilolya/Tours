Îles d'Orléans is a picturesque island just a short drive from Quebec City, known for its stunning landscapes and rich agricultural heritage. As you explore its charming villages, you'll find local artisan shops, delicious farm-to-table cuisine, and breathtaking views of the St. Lawrence River. On your way there, make sure to stop by Montmorency Falls, where the impressive cascade towers 83 meters high, creating a spectacular sight. The contrast between the serene island and the dramatic falls makes for an unforgettable journey, showcasing the natural beauty and cultural richness of the region.

Program

Start your day with a visit to Montmorency Falls, where you can marvel at the stunning 83-meter cascade and take a scenic walk along the boardwalk. After soaking in the views, head to Îles d'Orléans, where charming villages await. Enjoy local delicacies, explore artisan shops, and savor the breathtaking landscapes that highlight the island’s agricultural beauty. It’s a perfect blend of nature and culture for a memorable outing!
